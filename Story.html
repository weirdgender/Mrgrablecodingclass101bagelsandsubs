Family Owned Since 2005





Welcome to 101 Bagels and Subs — Oceanside’s Favorite Neighborhood Deli Located just minutes from the beach, 101 Bagels and Subs has been serving the Oceanside, California community with freshly made bagels, hearty subs, and locally roasted coffee since 2005. What started as a small family-run shop with a passion for New York–style bagels and California-inspired flavors has grown into a beloved breakfast and lunch spot for locals, surfers, and travelers alike. Every bagel is boiled and baked fresh each morning, and our subs are crafted to order with premium meats, crisp veggies, and house-made spreads. Whether you’re stopping by after a morning surf or grabbing lunch on your break, 101 Bagels and Subs is where Oceanside comes together for good food and good vibes.


Our Story and Commitment to the Community Founded by the Ramirez family, 101 Bagels and Subs began as a dream to bring authentic East Coast bagel traditions to the laid-back coastal culture of Southern California. Over the years, our little deli on Highway 101 has become a community staple — a place where friends meet, families gather, and visitors discover their new favorite sandwich. We’re proud to source ingredients from local California farms and bakeries whenever possible, supporting our neighbors and keeping everything fresh and sustainable. From classic breakfast bagels to signature subs like “The Oceanside Special,” every bite reflects our love for quality, community, and coastal living.


We have always been familiar with our locals and love meeting new customers to share our joy for food and comfort. We like to keep our shop calm and quiet for others who may need peace and quiet to rest and recharge their batteries. Unlike most restaurants, we like to keep it a casual environment for kids, groups of people, and of course our furry dog friends. We provide indoor and outdoor seating catering to your preferences. We hope to meet new customers and can’t wait for your arrival to try out our food and enjoy our safespace.
